There's more at Jewel Changi Aiport than you think! Apart from the well-known Rain Vortex, and butterfly gardens, there's actually a portion of Jewel called Canopy Park that not many know! Opened on 10 June 2020, this attraction has much to offer.

Canopy Mazes

There are 2 canopy mazes in Jewel. The hedge maze allows visitors to take a leisurely walk while the mirror maze creates a space of disorientation where visitors cna embark on a journey of exploration through carefully curated touch points.

Sky Nets

The Sky Nets were designed with two experiences in mind. The first is a walking net designed to introduce a slight thri9ll factor as it straddles a five-storey high void. Made with sturdy material, the walking net is fashioned like a contemplative walk in the park, but at more than 25 metres above ground.

The second net, the bouncing net, allows visitors to bounce their way amongst the trees in the canopy of Jewel. The fun level is higher than that of the walking net, and it is designed for longer dwell times. Its thrill factor comes in the form of an 8-metre high lookout at its highest point
