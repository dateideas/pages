Geylang Serai Ramadan Bazaar

Every year, many flock to the Geylang Serai Ramadan Bazaar to enjoy not just the food and the shopping but also the festive atmosphere.

However, this year due to the global pandemic, our yearly Bazaar has been cancelled. In such a situation, many merchants have chosen to adapt by going online! Who's to say we can't enjoy our favourite ramly burger or that creamy white rabbit milkshake?

Compiled for you is a list of online shops you can purchase from to satisfy that nutella bomb craving (;

gobaza.sg

Gobaza is by far one of the more user friendly websites that allows you to easily navigate through the many categories of products being featured. This is by far one of the better websites i've visited in terms of variety of products and also in terms of ease of navigation. On top of the usual food and fashion products, they also feature workshops and courses!

bazaarrayasg2020

bazaarrayasg2020 is a instagram page featuring vendor's listings in a clean and eye-catching square post. Most of their vendors are food vendors. so if you're missing the Geylang Serai Ramadan Bazaar food, then this is a must go to!

e-Bazaar

Visit e-Bazaar and their expansive products: from your favorite Hari Raya snacks (Susu cookies) to trendy hand-held bags waiting to be discovered.

Wisma Geylang Serai

Recently launched and spearheaded by Wisma Geylang Serai, Geylangbazaar.online is the place where you can shop and support small and medium business owners with little knowledge in eCommerce, ranging from eye-catching clothing, household items and kitchen electronics, to even health supplement products.
