Overcooked Co-op (Online)

Overcooked is yours for FREE if you download it now! This offer ends on 11th June 2020.

Overcooked is a cooking simulation game developed by Ghost Town Games and published by Team17. In a local cooperative experience, players control a number of chefs in kitchens filled with various obstacles and hazards to rapidly prepare meals to specific orders under a time limit.

The game is actually a co-op game that cannot be played online, however, our team has found a way you could enjoy this game with your loved ones online! Here's how to set it up.

Step 1: Download Overcooked

Download Overcooked

Step 2: Download Parsec - Online Platform

Download Parsec

Step 3: Add your loved ones on Parsec

Open up Parsec and click on the social icon on the left toolbar. Click on add friend and key in your loved one's user ID. (You can locate your user ID on the top right-hand corner)

Once added, click on their username under your friends list and select only "Keyboard Control".

Step 4: Host the game

Open up Overcooked and then Alt-tab to Parsec.

On Parsec, click the computer icon and click share. Those in your friend's list should receive a notification to join your game.

Unfortunately, you need to be running on Windows 8.1, Windows 10 or better to be able to host a game. You can still join a game hosted by your friends if you're not running on these systems.

Step 5: Play

Your loved ones should now have access to your game through the Parsec application. Change the controls in your game to allow for multiple players to play! For keyboard players, only a maximum of 2 players is allowed.

However, if your loved ones have a controller they can link to their computer, they can join and you can host up to 4 players!
