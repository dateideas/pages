Universal Studios Singapore and S.E.A Aquarium
$59 (USS) & $29 (S.E.A. Aquarium)
Ends 31 July 2020
Resorts World Sentosa
important

Only valid for Singapore Residents with valid Republic of Singapore issued birth certificate/Photo National ID/Work Permit/Student Pass. ID is required for entry.

Many places of attractions in Singapore are shutdown due to the Circuit Breaker measures but the Universal Studios Singapore and S.E.A. Aquarium is finally opened for all of us!

Universal Studios Singapore Adult One-Day Ticket at $59

Missing the adrenaline rush from all the different rides and attractions from Universal Studios Singapore? With the Adult One-Day Ticket price reduced to $59, take your date out to the theme park and create new memories at the different rides and areas of the theme park.

The park is currently operating from Thursday to Sunday, 2.00pm - 9.00pm. Do note that the park will be closed from Monday to Wednesday due to the change of operating hours.

Visit here to book now!

You can also top up $9 for priority entry to the park from 12pm - 2pm where you get access to exclusive Meet & Greet sessions and F&B outlets.

S.E.A. Aquarium Adult One-Day Ticket at $29

Craving for a more intimate and romantic date instead? Head over to the S.E.A. Aquarium instead.

Adult One-Day Tickets are currently priced at $29 (U.P. $41) and with the reduced crowd in the aquarium, it is certain that you and your date will be able to snap some couple photos without the usual photo-bombing crowd.

The S.E.A. Aquarium is currently operating from Saturday to Tuesday, 10.00am - 5.00pm. Do note that the aquarium will be closed from Wednesday to Friday due to the change of operating hours.

Visit here to book now!

Note: Discounted tickets are only valid for visits to the both parks from 1 - 31 July 2020.
