Shilin Night Market

Shi Lin Ye Shi (Shilin Night Market) can be considered the quintessential of the Taiwanese food and culture and this year, our most favorite night carnival is back in Singapore with a twist.

No need for us to worry about missing out the night market experience as the fun is now brought online for all of us.

Here are the 4 virtual zones one can look forward to when visiting our digital Shilin Night Market!

EAT

Be prepared to be spoilt for choices as you navigate through all the local and regional food vendors available online.

SHOP

Choose from a variety of electronic products to fashion clothing and accessories on this huge digital marketplace.

PLAY

Of course, the experience will never be the same without games but rest assure that is it going to be equally exciting because all the games come with prizes!

FUN

With a series of programs and activities lined up for all of us throughout the 3 days on the Shilin Night Market official stage, we are sure the fun factor for is going to be very well taken care of.

The digital Shilin Night Market will be opened from 12-11pm on 19-21 June 2020.

Visit their instagram page for more information and updates on their program.
