Synopsis

Thomas Lim’s award-winning SUPERVISION tells the gripping story of a daughter clashing with her father over her questionable caregiving methods – with their domestic helper caught in between. Winner of Best Original Script at the 2019 Straits Times Life! Theatre Awards, SUPERVISION stars Janice Koh, Patrick Teoh and Umi Kalthum Ismail.

IMDA Rating: Advisory (Some Mature Content)
