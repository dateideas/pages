Virtual Disney World

It may come off as a bummer that we are all not able to travel this summer because of the COVID19 outbreak but it does not mean we are not able to experience the Disney World magic from the comfort of our home.

From Disney's best roller-coasters and family-friendly attractions to parades, and firework displays, engage your partner in a fun-filled magical day at home. Put on your VR gear (if you have) to maximise the magical experience.

Here are 3 experiences specially curated by our team to share with all of you:

Virtual Disney Rides

Start your day with a series of virtual disney rides compiled in a playlist by Disney Park. Be spoilt for choices from the 26 virtual rides available.

Disney Magic Happens Parade

Take a break from the rides as you sit back and relax. Sing along to the Disney hits with the casts of the Disneyland Park parade - Magic Happens.

Happily Ever After Fireworks Show

What can beat ending the day with a magical Disney fireworks show? Walk down the memory lane with Disney's musical performances from Moana, Frozen, The Little Mermaid, The Lion King, and more.

Experience the magic right in front of Cinderella Castle at the Walt Disney World Resort. Watch the castle transform into a canvas for some of your favorite characters and just like their stories, everything will turn out to be happily ever after!

Visit the Disney Park Youtube Channel to check out the full list of rides and experiences available.
