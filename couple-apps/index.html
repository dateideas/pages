Apps for Couples

Stay connected with your other half through the different apps available!

Rave

Rave allows you to watch videos and listen to music with your significant other at the same time. You can watch videos on Reddit, YouTube, and more, and be able to message back and forth in real time.

Happy Couple

Happy Couple is a quiz game for couples to learn more about one another. The daily quiz features 10 new questions each day which is great for couples to stay connected and understand each other better in tough times.

Get it on iOS here Get it on Playstore here

Kast

Kast, similar to Rave, is a comprehensive chat and sharing app supporting video, voice, text etc but also can be used to host movie and TV parties

Loklok

LokLok is a connected lock-screen that is always in-sync with you and your other half. You can draw, write messages, or add photos to your screen, and it shows up on their screens.
