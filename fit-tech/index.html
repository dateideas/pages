What is EMS Training?

EMS (electrical muscle stimulation) is a high-intense whole-body workout uses low-frequency electrical impulses to stimulate those muscles that are often ignored by conventional training methods.

During an EMS training session, which only lasts about 20 minutes, all big groups of muscles are stimulated, also the underlying muscles.

Despite its short duration, EMS is as effective as several hours of traditional strength training.
